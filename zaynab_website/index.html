Lazy Cake Recipe

Lazy Cake is one of the easiest cakes to make, yet is very tasty. It only has 4 ingredients and takes very little time to make.
Ingredients:
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Biscuits



Directions:
  1. First, melt the unsalted butter
  2. Put the melted butter, cocoa powder, and condensed milk into a bowl and mix
  3. Crush the biscuits into fine chunks and add them into the mixture, if preferred, you can add walnuts with the biscuits.
  4. Put this mixture either in a loaf pan or rap it up in aluminum foil into a cylinder shape and then freeze it for about 4 hours.

  5. After 4 hours take it out and let it thaw for a few minutes, cut it into rectangular slices, and enjoy!
